Radio

The "Making Your Life Count" radio program, originally known as "The Lighthouse Report," has been broadcast to millions for many years running now. Each short, yet packed, program offers practical, relevant approaches and ideas through interviews with people just like you who are making their lives count for more. These ideas are designed to both inspire and help you to extend your influence for Christ to your extended communities. We are currently in conversations to expand our radio presence to new stations around the globe.
