Make a Difference

Do you want your life to count for something lasting and worthwhile? Most people today want more from their lives than just being successful in their profession or making money. People want to have meaningful, lasting relationships that in some way benefit others around them and beyond. We have seen, for instance, how people show up by the thousands to help those afflicted by natural disasters or in support of some other worthy cause.

No doubt you want to make a difference. I would like to give you a free download of "Make Your Life Count." You will see how it is God’s desire that we are used in amazing ways to make a difference for Him.

You can do it—if you follow God’s plan and operate in His power! By walking with God, loving your neighbors and sharing with others.

People Like You!

Colin
Washington State University

He met with two Christians and said, “I need to change my life.” After choosing Christ some of his fraternity brothers had taken note of the differences in his attitude and in his behaviors. Curious about Colin’s experience, they were beginning to ask questions of him about the Christian life. The foundation for a life of impact is a life-changing WALK with God.

Hrvoje
Croatia

A professor sought help for first-year students from his friend Hrvoje Malovic, who leads a Christian group on campus. Hrvoje told the professor that he had a study-skills seminar that could help. Because the professor had seen how Hrvoje had shown genuine care and love for the students, he was willing to allow Hrvoje and seven other Christian workers to instruct students in how to organize their time better and improve their study habits. Now there is a growing cooperation to offer the seminar to the 500-plus freshmen.

Anna
North Africa

Growing up in North Africa, Anna had been repeatedly sexually abused. As a student she passed by a house with a small sign that said, “Jesus is love.” In her pain she cried out, “Reveal yourself to me, ‘Jesus of love.’” In a dream Jesus said to Anna: “Accept Me as your Lord and Savior.” The people in the home gave her a DVD that presents the story of Jesus through the lenses of women. Anna watched it three times, responded to Christ, and couldn’t stop crying for two days.

She led her mother and sister to the Lord as well as many fellow students. Others continued to boldly tell others about Jesus.
