Brief Overview  Jump to Start

A Life of Significance

Walk

Increase your grasp of how God wants you to live. Demonstrate credibility. Walk with Him so others see your actions match your words.

Pray

Talk with God about things that matter to you and others. Nothing is too small or big for Him. Connect closely with God and receive His guidance and power.

Care

Look for ways to be conscious of the continual needs of others. Demonstrate love and kindness freely and frequently.

Share

You have made your life count, now help others to experience the same. Team up with others to spread the message of Christ’ love to the world.
